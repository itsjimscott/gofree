Types of Monopoly Go Events

1. Daily & Weekly Events

Monopoly Go refreshes events daily and weekly, giving players consistent opportunities to earn rewards:

  • Cash Grab: Land on Chance tiles to trigger a cash-collecting mini-game (up to 10x multipliers).
  • Free Parking: Accumulate dice by landing on marked tiles, then cash in when you hit Free Parking.
  • Golden Blitz: Trade golden stickers with friends during this exclusive window.
  • Peg-E Prize Drop: Drop tokens to win dice, cash, and sticker packs.

2. Flash Events

These short, intense events require quick action:

  • Builder's Bash: Save on landmark upgrades for 30 minutes.
  • Mega Heist: Earn boosted rewards from Bank Heists.
  • Sticker Boom: Gain 50% more stickers from packs.

3. Special Tournaments

Compete globally for leaderboard prizes:

  • Rent Frenzy: Collect rent from more properties.
  • Landmark Rush: Earn extra rewards for upgrading landmarks.
  • Tundra Treasures Dig: Unearth rewards like 4,000+ dice rolls (active until February 4, 2025).

Pro Tips for Dominating Monopoly Go Events

Plan Ahead

  • Check the in-game schedule daily for events like Wheel Boost (extra spins) or Cash Boost (double earnings).
  • Set reminders for time-sensitive tournaments like Golden Blitz.

Maximize Rewards

  • Use multipliers strategically during Cash Grab or High Roller events.
  • Pair Builder's Bash with Landmark Rush to save cash and earn bonuses.

Collaborate with Friends

  • Team up during Partners Events to tackle milestones faster.
  • Share tips via community platforms to stay updated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often do Monopoly Go events refresh?

A: Daily events like Cash Grab reset every 12-24 hours, while tournaments like Rent Frenzy last 1-2 days.

Q: Can I trade stickers outside Golden Blitz?

A: No-golden stickers are tradeable only during Golden Blitz events.

Q: Are Flash Events worth the effort?

A: Absolutely! Events like Mega Heist offer 2x rewards for Bank Heists, accelerating your progress.
